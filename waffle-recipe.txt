Ingredients:
 - 2 tablespoons brown sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups rolled oats
 - 1 cup flour
 - 1/4 cup butter (melted)
 - 2 tablepoons baking powder
 - 1 tablespoon cinnamon
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/2 cup milk

Directions:
 1. Preheat waffle iron. Mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients seperately.
 
 2. Combine ingredients.
 
 3. Add 1/3 cup of waffle mix to iron and close lid. Waffle is done when the
 top is golden brown an the edges are formed.
